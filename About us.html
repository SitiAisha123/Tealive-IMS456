About Us


HERE’S THE TEA ABOUT TEALIVE


Tealive began its breakthrough journey as a humble tea brand from Malaysia. Today, we are Southeast Asia’s largest lifestyle tea brand with over 800 outlets around the world! Still, one thing remains true to us – our quest to bring joy through tea. From serving freshly-brewed beverages at our outlets to delivering our popular DIY bubble tea kits to your doorstep, you know you can count on Tealive for an unforgettable experience!
